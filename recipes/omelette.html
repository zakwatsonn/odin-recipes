Omelette

Description

This is a recipe on how to make an omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 1tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and season with salt and pepper
  2. Heat the pan to a medium heat and pour in the seasoned eggs
  3. Stir gently until the eggs are cooked through
  4. Fold the omelette and serve
